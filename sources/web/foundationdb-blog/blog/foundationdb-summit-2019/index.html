FoundationDB Summit 2019

Published July 23, 2019

We’re happy to announce that this year’s FoundationDB Summit will again be hosted in coordination with KubeCon+CloudNativeCon in San Diego, CA on November 18, 2019.

We’re looking for great speakers, and the call for proposals is now open! We welcome talks from existing and new community members, of varying backgrounds and experience with FoundationDB and public speaking. The CFP deadline is Friday, August 9.

Registration for attending the FDB Summit will open soon!
